Kindred Hospitals provide specialized acute care for patients with serious conditions and multiple comorbidities. With treatments available such as ventilator liberation, IV antibiotic therapy, wound debridement, and bedside dialysis, we are able to treat a wide variety of critical and chronic conditions. Our physicians, ACLS-certified nurses, respiratory therapists, rehabilitation specialists, and pharmacists are dedicated to helping our complex patients recover and return to the lives they love.
